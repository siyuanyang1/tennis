About Us

We are a dedicated group of athletes and advocates working towards gender equality in tennis. Our mission is to ensure that female tennis players receive fair treatment, equal pay, and the same opportunities as their male counterparts.

Issues

Female tennis players face various challenges, including unequal pay, limited media coverage, and fewer opportunities. Here are some key issues we are addressing:

  • Unequal pay: Despite similar or higher performance, female players often earn less than their male counterparts.
  • Media coverage: Women's matches receive less media coverage, even when they draw comparable or higher viewership.
  • Opportunities: Female players often have fewer opportunities to compete in high-profile tournaments.
